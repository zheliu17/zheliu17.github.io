Hi, nice to see you! I am a first year master student of the University of Alberta, Department of Chemistry, working with Prof. Gabriel Hanna.

I have a wide interest in theoretical and computational chemistry. I am currently doing theoretical works. We are developing the new dynamics methodologies of the mixed quantum-classical system, based on the quantum-classical Liouville equation (QCLE).

Before joining the University of Alberta, I did some projects in computational chemistry. My previous research includes the photocatalysis of Titanium dioxide, the ab initio molecule dynamic simulation of water and hydroxy radical solutions, the solvation process in Lithium battery electrolyte, and so forth. I am interested in almost everything in theoretical and computational, and always open to start a new research.

In these computational projects, I used VASP, CPMD, VMD and Gaussian under Linux operating systems. I also have the experience in MATLAB, Python and Latex. I will use Fortran in coming month.

I received my Bachelor of Science degree from the Dalian University of Technology, Zhang Dayu school of Chemistry . I am very proud of being a member in this class. I was also a visiting student at Dalian Institute of Chemical Physics, CAS, (Advisor, Prof. Hongjun Fan) and the University of Calgary (Mitacs Globalink Program, Advisor, Prof. Peter Kusalik).
